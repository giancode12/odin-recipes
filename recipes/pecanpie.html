Meemaw's Pecan Pie

Description

The pecan tree in Meemaw’s front yard blanketed the ground with nuts every fall, so it’s no surprise that this was her preferred Thanksgiving dessert.

The recipe is similar to the classic one you’ll find on the Karo Corn Syrup bottle, but there are some notable differences

Ingredients

  • 1 cup light corn syrup
  • 3 large eggs, beaten
  • 1-2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons butter or Oleo melted
  • 1/2 cup chopped pecans
  • Unbaked pastry crust
  • 1/2 cup pecan halves

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Mix the corn syrup, eggs vanilla, sugar, salt, and butter or margarinein a large bowl.Stir the chopped pecans into the corn syrup mixture.
  3. Press the pastry crust into the bottom and up the sides of a 9-inch pie pan.Flute the edges.
  4. Pour the filling into the crust, then arrange the pecan halves on top.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes to an hour, or until the filling is set.
